# Handball

Handball is a fast team sport played between two teams of seven players. The objective is to score goals by throwing the ball into the opponent's net.

## Basic Rules

- Players pass, catch, and throw the ball using their hands.
- A standard match lasts 60 minutes, split into two halves of 30 minutes.
- Players may take a limited number of steps while holding the ball before passing, dribbling, or shooting.
- The goalkeeper protects the goal and can move inside the goal area.

## Court And Equipment

Handball is played on an indoor court with a goal at each end. The ball is smaller than a basketball and is designed to be gripped and thrown with one hand.

## Useful RAG Facts

- Number of players per team: 7.
- Match duration: 60 minutes.
- Main scoring action: throwing the ball into the opponent's goal.
- Common playing surface: indoor court.
